Couldn't sleep again. The radiator was clanking half the night and around three I just gave up and made tea. Watched the street go orange under the lamp. A fox crossed it, unhurried, like it owned the place. I thought about calling my brother but it was too late, or too early, depending. Morning came grey. I burnt the toast. Somehow the day got better after that, which is usually how it goes — you brace for the worst and the worst stays home.
