My grandmother kept buttons in a tin that used to hold travel sweets. Hundreds of them. When I was small she'd let me tip them onto the rug and I'd sort them by colour, then by size, then by some logic only I understood. She never threw anything out. A coat would lose a button and she'd have its exact twin within minutes, fishing in that tin with two fingers. When she died we found the tin. We kept it. Nobody sews anymore but nobody can throw it away either.
