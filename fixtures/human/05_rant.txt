Look, I get that everyone loves this show but I just don't see it. Three seasons in and the plot still hasn't decided what it wants to be. Great acting, sure. Gorgeous to look at, fine. But every episode ends on a cliffhanger that gets resolved in about four boring minutes the next week, and I'm tired. My partner keeps saying it gets good in season four. That's twenty more hours of my life. Twenty! For "it gets good." No thanks.
